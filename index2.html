QR Phishing Awareness

This project demonstrates the dangers of QR-based phishing attacks (Quishing).

⚠️ What is Quishing?

Quishing is a type of phishing attack where malicious QR codes redirect users to harmful websites to steal data.

🚨 Common Risks

  • Fake login pages
  • Data theft (passwords, OTP)
  • Malware downloads

✅ Safety Tips

  • Verify the source of QR codes
  • Avoid scanning unknown codes
  • Check URLs before entering data
